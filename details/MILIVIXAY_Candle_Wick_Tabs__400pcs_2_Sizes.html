MILIVIXAY Candle Wick Tabs

Whether you're a passionate candle maker or an experienced DIY crafter, MILIVIXAY Candle Wick Tabs are an essential addition to your toolkit. These 400 pieces of durable metal tabs are designed to fit various wick sizes, making them perfect for both small and large candle projects. The tabs come in two versatile sizes: 20mm and 12mm, allowing you to match them to the exact dimensions of your candle wicks.

Key Features

  • Two sizes (20mm & 12mm) for versatile wick support
  • Sturdy metal construction for long-lasting durability
  • Perfect fit for all wick types and thicknesses
  • Help reduce candle wax waste with precise sizing
  • Designed for use in both DIY and custom candle making

Why Choose These Tabs?

These multi-purpose wick tabs are engineered to prevent hassle and waste during the candle-making process. They allow you to manage multiple wicks effectively in a single candle, with the tabs fitting snugly without overlapping or being too wide. For instance, a large candle requiring multiple wicks can benefit from the smaller 12mm tabs, while a single, thick wick can be held firmly with the 20mm version.

The tabs are also designed to enhance the efficiency of your candle-making efforts by minimizing the use of excess materials. A useful tip is to apply melted wax underneath the tabs for extra stability and to ensure they stay in place throughout the process. This not only improves the quality of your finished candle but also helps you save on supplies with every project.
